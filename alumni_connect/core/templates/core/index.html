What is AluLink?

AluLink is more than a platform; it's the bridge between your academic journey and your professional future. We provide an exclusive, AI-powered network designed solely for the students and alumni of our institution.

Our mission is to foster a community where experienced alumni can mentor the next generation, and where students can find the guidance, support, and direct referrals needed to launch successful careers. It's networking, reimagined.
